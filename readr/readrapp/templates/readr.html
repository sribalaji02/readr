User Guide

How to interact with Readr.?
Use "I need a book" or "Can you recommend some books" to request books from Readr.
Click "Next" to see the next five books.
Use "Download book (book index number)" or "I want book (book index number)" to get download links for your chosen books.
Use "I need some details about book (book index)" or "Compare books" to obtain book details or to compare books.
Click the below icon to start a conversation
